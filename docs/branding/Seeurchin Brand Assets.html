Usage

Typeface
Baloo 2 SemiBold for the wordmark · always lowercase “seeurchin”.
Min mark size
16 px digital. Below ~14 px, use the mono ink cut.
Clear space
Keep one spike-length of padding clear on all sides (shown right).
On photos
Use the reversed (white) cut, or place on the ocean squircle.
The hatched margin equals the radius of one spike — the minimum breathing room around the mark in any lockup.
